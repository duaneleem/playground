Are you a VIP? Changes thinks you are!

Pampering your mind, body & spirit at Changes is rewarding in and of itself, but now you can earn BIG SAVINGS on any of our world-class services and products by enrolling in our Changes VIP Program. It's our way of thanking you for your continued patronage.

How it Works:

Every time you purchase Products or Gift Certificates, Refer a new client, purchase a series, try a new service, pre-book, or attend a special Changes event, you earn & accumulate VIP POINTS.
  • Earn Points for a product or gift certificate purchase (1x purchase amount)
  • Earn Points for referring a friend (500 VIP Points)
  • Earn Points trying a new service (100 VIP Points)
  • Earn Points for pre-booking a future appointment before leaving the spa (100 VIP Points)
  • Earn Points for attending special Changes events (points will vary)

How do I Redeem:

Ask our Front Desk or check the bottom of your receipt for your VIP balance and then decide if you are ready to use them that day or save them to try different Changes service. 100 points = $1.00, 500 points = $5.00, etc.

How to Enroll:

It's free to join. Simply call or visit the Front Desk and update your information when you are ready to enroll... then keep your eyes open for special e-mail blasts and signs around the spa for chances to earn extra points.

Terms & Conditions:

  • VIP Points have no cash value and are based on the purchase price of services and in-spa products, excluding taxes and gratuity.
  • VIP Points may not be redeemed for gratuities.
  • In order to enroll and earn points, client must update address, contact numbers and email address.
  • Points must be redeemed by 12/31 of the following year in which they are earned; ex. 2007 VIP Points are redeemable through 12/31/08.
  • Program rules and guidelines subject to change without prior notice
